Ill. 6: 

Measurement rate limited by calculation time

The time between two exposure periods is called the measurement repeat time, which can be
converted into a measurement frequency. The calculated measurement frequency indicates
how many measured values the sensor can output per second.

n

Formula for calculating the measurement frequency:

▪ Measurement frequency [kHz] = 1 / Measurement repeat time [ms]
