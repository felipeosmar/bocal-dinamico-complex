Troubleshooting

 CAUTION

Release of dangerous laser radiation when the sensor is defective.

Use of the sensor with a fractured front panel or loose/exposed laser optics can release danger-
ous laser radiation.

a) In case of a fractured front panel or loose/exposed laser optics disconnect the laser from the

power immediately.

b) Have the sensor checked by an authorised person (specialist). Do not operate the sensor

until that time.

sensor was changed and you can no longer connect to the sensor. Proceed as follows:
