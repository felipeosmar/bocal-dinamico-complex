Adjusting the internal resolution

Adjusting the internal resolution allows you to adjust the number of pixels transferred by the
camera. While this affects the measurement rate positively, it lowers the resolution and must
therefore be adjusted individually to the application. For this process, proceed as follows:

Condition:

ð

The 

Expert Mode

 is activated (see 

Header [

}

 35]

).

Instruction:

a) Select mode 

Parametrization

 | 

Global Parametrization

 in the menu.

b) In the 

Global Parameters

 window, proceed to 

Data Acquisition

 | 

Internal Resolution

.

c) In the drop-down list next to 

Number of points in x

, select the desired number of points

along the X axis that should be read out by the camera of the sensor.

d) In the drop-down list next to 

Binning in z

, select whether points along the Z axis should be

read out by the camera as a summary and as a combined point.
